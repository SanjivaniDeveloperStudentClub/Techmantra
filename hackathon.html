What is

TECHBLITZ?

Techblitz Hackathon is a 6-hour event scheduled to take place on 15th March at Sanjivani K.B.P Polytechnic in Kopargaon. The hackathon is being organized by the Department of Computer Technology under the Developer Student Club. Participants will be presented with problem statements in the domains of Web Application, Web Development, and Mobile Application Development. This event promises to be an exciting opportunity for tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills and creativity in a competitive and collaborative environment.
