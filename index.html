What is

TECHMANTRA?

Sanjivani Techmantra 2k24 is a technical event to be conducted at Sanjivani K. B. P. Polytechnic.It aims to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and innovation. Participants can engage in various challenges and competitions to test their knowledge and problem-solving abilities. The event promises to be a stimulating and enriching experience for all the participants.
